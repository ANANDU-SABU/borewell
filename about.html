We are the leaders in bore well industry, specialises in professional bore well drilling throughout Kerala

Our company was established in 1990, and since its inception has undertaken various contracts for various types of bore well, calix, tube well, water exploration, dewatering boreholes, earth pits etc. The belief that customer satisfaction is as important as their products and services, have helped this establishment garner a vast base of customers, which continues to grow by the day.

Reena Borewells has a leading role in drilling industry with the dedicated, advanced and innovative staff and geologists who offer excellent drilling services which ensures accurate utilization of resources and give correct advises on availability, location, diameter, depth, purity and costs in a geological perspective. We provide a total drilling solution for our clients with all wide range of modern machineries. Reena Borewells is an inhouse specialist in super fast high power rigs. We also undertake installations; repairs of all types of Borewells, Calix, Rotary, Earth Point, Cleaning, Yield testing etc. Drilling is done in all soils including clay & rocks. Another peculiarity is we can offer Borewells where the digging point is 600 meters away from the road. Reena borewells is a registered drilling contractor with the Ground water Department Kerala proving integrated solutions from well design to completion in various surfaces and underground conditions. Reena borewells owns a large fleet with a rated capacity up to 2000 Ft. Our services has been associated with the various types and diameter of borewell / tubewell / handbore drilling and rig supply in industry of South India for over 30 years. We supply all our rigs and modern drilling and survey equipments to all other contractors who work in Kerala. For this all our percussion tools, backed with support vehicles and ancillary equipments, staffs and machinery are working in a 24 x 7 manner even in shifts.

EXPERIENCE

Reena borewells has completed many government contracts for the supply of clean drinking water for Schools, hospitals and many public rural and urban residential/agriculture/water projects. We have completed several contracts for water boreholes/wells, monitoring and exploration for several private flats/apartments/residential villas. Our 6x6 and 4x4 vehicles allow us to access the most rugged and difficult of terrains. Furthermore, we offer after sales service complying with customer convenience and preference. Guided by the spirit of “ dynamism, loyalty, honesty and commitment”, we strictly follow the core values of “credibility, harmony, innovation and safety and being performance-driven”. We are committed to providing services that enhance and optimize our clients’ performance with no accidents, no harm to people and no damage to the environment.
